Types of home appliances
Green
Washing Machine
Washing machines and dryers are another significant source of energy consumption in our households. With advancements in technology, manufacturers have introduced features that help reduce their environmental impact. When shopping for a washing machine, opt for models with adjustable load sizes and shorter wash cycles. These options allow you to customize the water and energy usage based on the size of your laundry load, helping you save resources.
Solar
Water heaters
Solar-powered water heaters offer a sustainable solution for providing hot water in homes. These innovative appliances utilise the sun's energy to heat water, reducing reliance on traditional energy sources.
Eco
Refrigerators
Refrigerators are one of the most energy-consuming appliances in our homes. They run 24/7, keeping our food fresh and our beverages cold. When selecting an eco-friendly refrigerator, look for those with the Energy Star label, which signifies high energy efficiency. These models often incorporate advanced technologies such as improved insulation, LED lighting, and optimized cooling systems.
Sustainable
Dishwashers
Dishwashers have come a long way in terms of energy efficiency. Modern models are designed to save water and electricity while still delivering excellent cleaning performance. When shopping for a dishwasher, look for models with eco-friendly features, such as energy-saving modes and adjustable water pressure. These options allow you to customize the dishwasher's settings based on the load size and level of dirtiness, optimizing the energy and water usage.
